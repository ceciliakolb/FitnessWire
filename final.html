About

Every year, at least 2.8 million people worldwide die as a result of being overweight or obese, with up to 400,000 deaths in the US alone. Our goal is to change that statistic. With easy access to the nearest health food restaurants, gyms, and top-notch personal trainers, anyone can get (and keep) their life on the right, healthy track. Instead of making it a 3 month “diet”, make it a lifestyle. That is what FitnessWire® is all about.

Our #1 intention is to promote a healthy, active lifestyle. We’re here to show you that being fit doesn’t have to be difficult. Especially in the Austin area, fitness has become a growing trend. Supermarkets like WholeFoods, Sprouts, and Trader Joe’s make cheap, nutritious foods accessible to nearly everyone. Utilize the resources on this site to your advantage to become your healthiest, happiest, self. We believe in you!
